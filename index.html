How to Build

how to build a printable mask

We supply the templates and instructions that enable you to turn a piece of cardboard into beautiful 3D masks that you can build and decorate yourself. The instructions are easy-to-follow so that anyone can take part in the assembling process, using local materials and removing the need for mass manufacturing and shipping.

See how easy it is to assemble a mask! With a few basic supplies that you may already have at home, you can be wearing a polygon mask in just a couple of hours!
